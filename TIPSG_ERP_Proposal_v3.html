01 Executive Summary

TIPS Globeducate currently operates a fragmented digital infrastructure — six standalone Vidyalaya School ERP portals, a WordPress website with no ERP integration, and no group-level reporting across its eight campuses. N2P Systems proposes a structured five-phase ERP Transformation and Digital Integration engagement to consolidate, connect, and future-proof TIPS Globeducate's technology estate under TIPS Microsoft Custom ERP Solution.

Context & Objectives
Why this engagement, and what it is designed to achieve

TIPS Globeducate has built a strong multi-campus presence across Tamil Nadu, Kerala, Karnataka, and Telangana — offering IB/IBDP, Cambridge IGCSE, CBSE, Kindercare, and Inclusive programmes. As the group scales under the Globeducate international network, the limitations of the current technology estate are becoming a structural constraint on growth, reporting, and the parent experience.

2.1 Engagement Objectives

  • → Replace six fragmented Vidyalaya ERP instances with a single TIPS Microsoft Custom ERP Solution implementation serving all campuses
  • → Integrate the group website (tipsglobeducate.com) with the ERP — enabling enquiry capture, admissions funnelling, and event data flow
  • → Deliver a consolidated group leadership dashboard across all eight campuses
  • → Migrate all student, fee, and academic data from existing Vidyalaya instances into TIPS ERP with zero data loss
  • → Establish N2P Systems as the primary managed service partner for all ERP and digital infrastructure needs post-go-live

3.4 Why This As-Is State Makes the Case for TIPS ERP

Vidyalaya is a displacement candidate
Vidyalaya is a mid-market Gujarat SaaS ERP built for standalone schools, not multi-campus international school groups. As TIPS Globeducate scales under Globeducate network, it will outgrow Vidyalaya's architectural ceiling within 2–3 years.
The silo gap is a boardroom problem
TIPS leadership currently has no consolidated view of group enrolment, fee collection, staff headcount, or academic KPIs. Six independent Vidyalaya portals generate six independent reports with no roll-up — a problem TIPS ERP solves on day one of go-live.
Every admissions lead is leaking
Enquiry forms on tipsglobeducate.com have no downstream system. Every parent who fills an enquiry form on the website goes into a manual process — or is lost. A TIPS ERP CRM module integration captures and qualifies every lead automatically.
Current spend funds the ROI argument
At ₹38–62 L/year on fragmented, siloed tools, a TIPS ERP implementation that consolidates ERP, comms, and website integration can be positioned as cost-neutral at scale — especially as Vidyalaya licences are phased out post-migration.
